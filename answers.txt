
Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We may consider using Git for monitoring version history, platforming collaboration, having online copy of work and is overall a great tool.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science could be applied to healthcare. For example, we can use methods learned from data science to analyze and predict global and local epidemics.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging creates a new merge commit that saves history and rebasing overwrites history by moving the feature branch onto the main branch, rewriting commit history.
